About us 

                Welcome to our restaurant, where farm-fresh flavors come to life!
Our story began with a shared passion for agriculture and a love for the vibrant taste of
fresh oranges. As partners, we cultivated our own orange farm, dedicating ourselves to
growing the juiciest, most flavorful oranges around. Inspired by the natural goodness of
our harvest, we dreamed of creating a place where others could enjoy the fruits of our
labor in a truly unique and delicious way.


That's how our restaurant was born. We wanted to offer more than just a meal—we
wanted to provide an experience that connects you directly to the source of your
food. Here, you can stroll through our beautiful orchard, handpick your own oranges, and then
enjoy them as a key ingredient in our delectable dishes. Our menu is crafted to
highlight the fresh, vibrant flavors of our farm, from zesty appetizers and crisp salads to
mouthwatering entrees and sweet, citrus-infused desserts.

We believe in the farm-to-table philosophy, ensuring that every bite you take is as fresh and flavorful as
possible. Our commitment to quality and sustainability drives everything we do, from our farming
practices to our culinary creations.

Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a special dinner, or simply to enjoy the orchard’s beauty, we
invite you to share in our passion for fresh, wholesome food. Join us and taste the difference that comes
from a farm rooted in love and a restaurant dedicated to exceptional dining experiences.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We look forward to serving you!


Warmly,

Brent and Darlene
